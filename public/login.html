About TDB4SD

The Theatre Database 4 San Diego is your place to find out everything about the theatre performances here in San Diego. Which show played where? When did it run? Who was in it? What awards did it win? You can find out here. Just put your search in, set any filters you want, and click Search. You can then click on the buttons in the results to chase down more specifics.

But, we need your info. If you have done a show that you want in the database, please send us an email. We'll need to know:

  • Theatre
  • Title
  • Author/Composer/Lyricist/Book
  • Open/Close Dates
  • Number of Performances
  • All the people involved and what they did (producer, director, tech, cast, orchestra, etc.
  • Any awards the show may have won (award, category, who, year)

If you have any questions or concerns, contact us.
